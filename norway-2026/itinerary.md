## 🗺️ Complete Daily Itinerary & Custom Activities

### ✈️ Day 1: Wednesday, July 15 – Transatlantic Departure
* **Transportation:** Flight out of Phoenix Sky Harbor (PHX).
  * **Bala & Reshma:** American Airlines Flight 2300 (PHX 8:54 AM ➔ PHL 4:42 PM) connecting to Flight 722 (PHL 6:25 PM ➔ DUB 6:30 AM).
  * **Kavya:** American Airlines Flight 3073 (PHX 8:30 AM ➔ BNA 2:02 PM) connecting to Aer Lingus Flight 76 (BNA 6:35 PM ➔ DUB 8:35 AM).
* **Activities:** Check in early, pass through security, and prepare for our summer trip. Enjoy the flight over the Atlantic!

### 🇳🇴 Day 2: Thursday, July 16 – Welcome to Oslo
* **Transportation:** Aer Lingus Flight EI 626 departing Dublin at 10:30 AM, landing at Oslo Gardermoen (OSL) at 1:40 PM. Board the ultra-smooth *Flytoget* high-speed train straight to Oslo S Central Station.
* **Activities:** * Check into **Citybox Oslo** and drop our bags.
  * Spend a sunny summer afternoon exploring **Aker Brygge** boardwalks and the outdoor modern sculpture gardens of **Tjuvholmen**.
  * Walk down to the scenic harbor promenade for our first evening meal in Norway.
* **Lodging Base:** Citybox Oslo (Prinsens Gate 6) | Ref: `CBO136672`

### 🏔️ Day 3: Friday, July 17 – The Glacial High Peaks
* **Transportation:** Vy Train F4 departing Oslo S at **6:23 AM sharp**, arriving at the mountain outpost of Finse at 10:46 AM (Car 2, Seats 43, 44, 47).
* **Activities:** * Drop backpacks at the remote mountain lodge.
  * Trek across otherworldly rock paths, rushing glacial streams, and summer snowfields toward the majestic **Hardangerjøkulen Glacier**.
  * Gather back at the warm lodge for a traditional three-course communal feast alongside hikers from across the globe.
* **Lodging Base:** Finsehytta DNT Mountain Lodge | Order: `20623` (Full Board Included)

### 🚴‍♂️ Day 4: Saturday, July 18 – The Iconic Rallarvegen Trail & Flåmsbana
* **Transportation:** * **Bala & Kavya:** Pick up mountain bikes at Finse and cycle **23 miles (38 km)** along the famous gravel **Rallarvegen Path** down to Myrdal Station. 
  * **Reshma:** Board the 11:20 AM mountain train to Myrdal, connecting immediately to the **12:06 PM Flåmsbana (Flåm Railway)** descent. Enjoy a peaceful window view down the canyon wall, stopping at the thundering **Kjosfossen Waterfall**.
  * **Family Reunion:** Regroup at the Flåm docks by 1:30 PM for fresh cardamom rolls and local coffee at *Bakern i Flåm*. Take a quick local regional bus into Lærdal valley.
* **Lodging Base:** Lindstrøm Hotel Lærdal (Øyraplassen 3) | Ref: `34611SG014071`

### 🪵 Day 5: Sunday, July 19 – Valley Histories & Salmon Rivers
* **Transportation:** Local cruiser or hybrid bikes rented at the property.
* **Activities:** * Cycle up a flat, beautiful path tracing the crystal-clear waters of the **Lærdalselvi Salmon River**.
  * Visit the world-famous **Borgund Stave Church**, a masterpiece of tiered wooden architecture standing proudly since 1180.
  * Spend a quiet afternoon strolling past the beautifully preserved, vibrant 18th-century storefronts of Old Lærdal.
* **Lodging Base:** Lindstrøm Hotel Lærdal | Ref: `34611SG014071`

### 🚢 Day 6: Monday, July 20 – The Majestic Nærøyfjord Cruise
* **Transportation:** Local morning bus to Flåm. Fjord Cruise Electric Vessel (10:15 AM Flåm ➔ 12:15 PM Gudvangen). Return Tunnel Shuttle Bus (3:45 PM Gudvangen ➔ 4:05 PM Flåm). Evening express coach down to Hardangerfjord.
* **Activities:**
  * Lock backpacks in the Flåm station storage lockers to hike hands-free.
  * Cruise on a completely silent, eco-friendly catamaran through the vertical 3,000-foot walls of the UNESCO-protected **Nærøyfjord**.
  * Step off in the ancient valley of **Gudvangen**. Explore the interactive paths of the *Viking Village Njardarheimr* and hike deep valley trails where you can stand directly behind curtains of roaring water.
* **Lodging Base:** Thon Hotel Sandven Norheimsund (Kaien 28) | Ref: `1012240510`

### 🍏 Day 7: Tuesday, July 21 – Hardanger Orchard E-Biking
* **Transportation:** Local rental e-bikes.
* **Activities:**
  * Glide along the peaceful fjord roads cutting straight through miles of sweet-smelling **apple, cherry, and plum orchards**.
  * Cycle out to the unique **Steinsdalsfossen Waterfall**, where a safe, paved path lets your family walk completely behind the roaring, cascading falls without a drop touching you.
  * Enjoy a relaxed, historic evening overlooking the waterfront from our hotel veranda.
* **Lodging Base:** Thon Hotel Sandven | Ref: `1012240510`

### 🥾 Day 8: Wednesday, July 22 – The Great Canyon Chasm
* **Transportation:** Excursion to the waterfall canyon, followed by an afternoon regional coach ride back over the mountain pass into Oslo.
* **Activities:**
  * Visit the breathtaking **Vøringsfossen Waterfall**. Stroll across suspended architectural viewing platforms looking straight down a 600-foot deep vertical chasm at the roaring glacial torrent.
  * Check into our central Oslo hub, then enjoy a late evening dinner in the trendy, artsy neighborhood of **Grünerløkka**.
* **Lodging Base:** Anker Hotel Oslo (Storgata 55) | Itinerary: `73467623265654`

### 🛴 Day 9: Thursday, July 23 – Oslo Harbor Cruise & Roof Walks
* **Transportation:** Local app-based e-scooter or e-bike rentals.
* **Activities:**
  * Zip effortlessly down Oslo's continuous harbor promenade.
  * Stroll right up the sloping, angular white marble roof of the modern **Oslo Opera House** for a panoramic view of the islands.
  * Spend an active afternoon walking among the dramatic bronze and granite human masterworks inside **Vigeland Sculpture Park**.
* **Lodging Base:** Anker Hotel Oslo | Itinerary: `73467623265654`

### 🌲 Day 10: Friday, July 24 – The Deep Nordmarka Forest Trails
* **Transportation:** Metro line up the mountain; specialized trail hybrid bike rentals.
* **Activities:**
  * Ride the local metro straight up to the alpine overlook at **Frognerseteren**.
  * Grab our trail bikes and dive into the endless network of perfectly groomed gravel logging tracks weaving past mirror-like lakes inside the **Nordmarka Wilderness**.
  * Stop by a traditional log cabin hidden in the deep pine woods for hot coffee and wild blueberry pastries before a thrilling downhill coast to the city.
* **Lodging Base:** Anker Hotel Oslo | Itinerary: `73467623265654`

### ✈️ Day 11: Saturday, July 25 – Heading Home to Phoenix
* **Transportation:** Morning *Flytoget* express to Oslo Airport. Return Flights: Aer Lingus/AA (Ref: `HWHAVC` / `2DCRS6`).
  * **OSL ➔ DUB:** 1:35 PM ➔ 2:55 PM (EI 627)
  * **DUB ➔ PHL:** 4:10 PM ➔ 6:55 PM (AA 7991)
  * **PHL ➔ PHX:** 9:50 PM ➔ 11:59 PM (AA 2721)
* **Activities:** Relax on the flight home, flip through our trip photos, and enjoy landing back in Phoenix!
